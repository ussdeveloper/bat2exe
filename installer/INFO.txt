bat2exe v1.2.0
====================

Repository: https://github.com/ussdeveloper/bat2exe
License: MIT + Apache 2.0 (MDI Icons)

Description:
  Convert .bat files with meta tags into standalone
  .exe executables. No runtime dependencies.

New in v1.2.0:
  🎨 Built-in Icon Picker (WPF) with 120+ MDI icons
  🌈 Color selector with presets
  ▶️ Icon picker opens automatically during conversion

Quick start:
  bat2exe script.bat         → script.exe (with icon picker)
  bat2exe --no-pick-icon     → skip icon picker

For help: bat2exe --help
