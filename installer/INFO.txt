bat2exe v1.1.0
====================

Repository: https://github.com/ussdeveloper/bat2exe
License: MIT

Description:
  Convert .bat files with meta tags into standalone
  .exe executables. No runtime dependencies.

Quick start:
  bat2exe script.bat         → script.exe
  bat2exe -input a.bat -o a.exe

For help: bat2exe --help
